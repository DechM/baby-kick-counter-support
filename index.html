Baby Kick Counter & Contractions

Welcome to the support page for Baby Kick Counter & Contractions. We're here to help you use the app effectively and answer any questions you may have.

Getting Started

Baby Kick Counter & Contractions is designed to be simple and intuitive:

  • Count Baby Kicks: Tap "Start Kick Session" and tap the button each time you feel a kick
  • Time Contractions: Tap "Start Contraction" when a contraction begins, then "End Contraction" when it ends
  • View History: Access your past sessions and contractions from the History screen
  • Settings: Customize your app preferences, set your due date, and more

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I count baby kicks?

Start a kick session from the Home screen. Each time you feel your baby move, tap the "+ Record Kick" button. The app will track the total number of kicks and the time between them. You can end the session manually or continue until you reach 10 kicks.

How do I time contractions?

Tap "Start Contraction" when a contraction begins. When it ends, tap "End Contraction". The app automatically calculates the duration and the interval since your last contraction.

What's the difference between Free and PRO?

Free: Full kick counter, full contraction timer, session summaries, and history for the last 7 days.

PRO: Unlimited history, export sessions as PDF/CSV, backup & restore, dark mode, and advanced summaries with trends.

How do I restore my PRO subscription?

If you've purchased PRO on another device or after reinstalling the app, tap "Restore Purchases" on the Premium screen. This will restore your subscription if it's still active.

Is my data safe?

Yes! All your data is stored locally on your device only. We don't collect, transmit, or share any of your data. No accounts required means no data on servers.

Can I export my data?

Yes! PRO users can export their kick sessions and contractions as PDF (formatted for your doctor) or CSV (raw data). You can also create backups to restore later.

What if I lose my data?

If you're a PRO user, you can restore from a backup. For free users, we recommend upgrading to PRO to enable backup functionality. Always consult with your healthcare provider about important pregnancy data.
